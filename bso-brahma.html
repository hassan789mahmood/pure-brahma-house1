About This Variety

BSO Brahma

BSO Brahma is a premium variety held in high regard among experienced breeders. When produced from well-maintained lines, BSO birds exhibit the characteristic Brahma size, temperament, and structural definition expected from a serious breeding program.

We maintain BSO lines to the same standards as all our other varieties — selective pairing, limited production, and careful early management. When this variety returns to availability, it will be announced to waitlist members first.

  • Strong Brahma structural characteristics
  • Highly valued among serious breeders
  • Bred from properly managed, documented pairs
  • Limited batches — waitlist essential for priority access
