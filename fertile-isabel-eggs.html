What These Eggs Are

Fertile Isabel Brahma Eggs

Fertile eggs collected from our controlled Isabel Brahma breeding pens. These eggs represent the same genetic line as our chick batches — sourced from select breeding pairs maintained specifically for quality output.

Designed for experienced breeders who prefer to hatch their own stock and manage the process from incubation onward. Eggs are handled with care from collection through dispatch.

For the Serious Breeder

Carefully Managed Collection

Eggs are collected from controlled pens with documented pair management — not random flock eggs.

Ideal for Serious Breeders

If you have your own incubator setup and want to hatch from quality genetics, this is your option.

Structured Breeding Foundation

Build or expand your own Isabel Brahma program using our foundational genetic line.
