About This Variety

Blue Columbian Brahma

The Blue Columbian Brahma is among the rarest and most refined of the Brahma varieties. It features a soft blue-laced bodycolour with distinctive columbian markings — a combination that requires skilled, precise genetic management to maintain.

This is a variety for the committed breeder. Blue genetics require careful pair selection to prevent genetic drift and maintain the true blue-laced expression across generations.

  • Rare blue-laced plumage — highly sought after by serious collectors
  • Complex genetics requiring disciplined pairing
  • Exhibition-grade potential when bred to standard
  • Extremely limited availability — waitlist only when active
